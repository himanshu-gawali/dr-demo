Hip Surgery

Hip surgery involves the surgical intervention of the hip joint to address conditions such as arthritis, fractures, or other hip-related issues, aiming to alleviate pain and improve function.

Knee Surgery

Knee surgery is a medical procedure performed to address various conditions affecting the knee joint, including injuries, arthritis, or other orthopedic issues, with the goal of relieving pain and restoring function.

Shoulder Surgery

Limb reconstruction, including limb lengthening, is a surgical approach that aims to correct deformities, injuries, or discrepancies in the limbs through procedures such as osteotomy, fixation devices, and gradual lengthening, enhancing overall limb function and symmetry.

Ligaments Surgery

Foot and ankle surgery encompasses a range of procedures addressing conditions like fractures, deformities, or arthritis in the lower extremities, aiming to restore mobility and alleviate pain.

Osseointegration

Osseointegration is the process by which a prosthetic device, such as an artificial limb or dental implant, forms a strong and stable bond with the surrounding bone tissue, enhancing functional integration and durability.

Fracture Fixation

Fracture fixation involves the surgical stabilization of broken bones using internal or external devices, promoting proper alignment and enabling the healing process.
